Flow rate sensor using Arduino to measure liquid flow

  • The objective is to measure the liquid flow in the overhead tank, and vehicle fuel measurement while filling to know the fraud tolerance in petrol bunks. Flow rate sensor is used to measure the flow rate of fluid which is connected to the Arduino board. The liquid that is passing through the flow rate sensor pushes the fins of the rotor, causing it to rotate. The shaft of the rotor is connected to the Hall effect sensor. As this rotor rotates a voltage/pulse is induced and for every liter of liquid passing through it per minute it outputs into pulses. These pulses are measured using an Arduino and then calculate the flow rate in liters per hour.
